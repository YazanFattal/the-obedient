About The Obedient

The Obedient is a fictional nation identity project built around respect, symbolism, philosophy, flags, currency, and visual storytelling. It is presented as a digital book and national archive.

01

THE OBEDIENT

The national identity of The Obedient consists of four official state flags. Each mode represents a condition of the nation and its values.

02

THE FOUR STATE FLAGS

X Mode is the primary national flag. It unites the Grey, Black, and Red modes into one official symbol.
