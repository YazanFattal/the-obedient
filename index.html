Founding Principles of The Obedient Published

02 July 2026

Official Announcement

The Obedient has officially released its first set of guiding principles. These principles define the values of the nation and the responsibilities shared by every citizen.

  1. Accountability

    Everyone will pay for their actions. Be careful with your choices.

  2. The Value of Life

    Your life is important. You are part of society.

  3. Justice

    Murder, serious crimes, and severe harm are punished based on the crime.

  4. Equality

    Everyone is welcome, no matter their colour, religion, or race.

  5. The Real Currency

    The real currency of The Obedient is respect and love.
